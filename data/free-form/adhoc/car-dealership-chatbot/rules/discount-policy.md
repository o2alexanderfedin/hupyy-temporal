# Discount Policy Rules

## Discount Categories and Percentages

### 1. First-Time Buyer Discount
- **Eligibility**: Customer has never purchased a vehicle before
- **Discount**: 5% off MSRP
- **Category**: INCENTIVE
- **Code**: FTB-001

### 2. Military Discount
- **Eligibility**: Active duty, veteran, or military family member
- **Discount**: 5% off MSRP
- **Category**: INCENTIVE
- **Code**: MIL-001
- **Documentation Required**: Military ID or DD Form 214

### 3. Year-End Clearance
- **Eligibility**: Purchase made between December 15 and December 31
- **Discount**: 15% off MSRP
- **Category**: SEASONAL
- **Code**: YEC-001
- **Note**: Applies to current model year vehicles only

### 4. Trade-In Bonus
- **Eligibility**: Customer trading in a vehicle
- **Bonus**: $1,500 additional credit (applied on top of trade-in value)
- **Category**: TRADE-INCENTIVE
- **Code**: TIB-001
- **Note**: This is NOT a percentage discount; it's an additional credit

### 5. Competitor Price Match
- **Eligibility**: Customer provides verifiable quote from competitor
- **Discount**: Up to 10% off MSRP to match competitor pricing
- **Category**: COMPETITIVE
- **Code**: CPM-001
- **Documentation Required**: Written quote from competitor (dated within 7 days)
- **Restrictions**:
  - Competitor must be within 50 miles
  - Same make, model, trim, and year
  - Competitor price must be verifiable

## Maximum Discount Caps

### By Category
- **INCENTIVE discounts**: Combined maximum 10% off MSRP
- **SEASONAL discounts**: Maximum 15% off MSRP
- **COMPETITIVE discounts**: Maximum 10% off MSRP
- **TRADE-INCENTIVE**: Maximum $2,000 bonus credit

### Overall Maximum
- **Without Manager Approval**: Total discount cannot exceed 20% off MSRP
- **With Manager Approval**: Total discount cannot exceed 30% off MSRP
- **With Regional Director Approval**: Total discount cannot exceed 35% off MSRP

## Calculation Order

1. Apply trade-in value as credit (reduces net price)
2. Calculate percentage discounts on MSRP (not reduced price)
3. Apply trade-in bonus as additional credit
4. Subtract all discounts and credits from MSRP to get final price

## Pricing Floor

- **Dealer Invoice Price**: Cannot sell below dealer invoice price without regional director approval
- **Minimum Margin**: Dealership must maintain at least 2% margin on all sales
